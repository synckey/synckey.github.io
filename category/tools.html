2015-05-20 Beijing 

vim ~/.ssh/config

贴入如下内容:

Host *
ServerAliveInterval 30
ControlMaster auto
ControlPath ~/.ssh/master-%r@%h:%p
ControlPersist yes

退出iterm2，重新登录即可。
